History

The Zambia Institute of Business Studies and Industrial Practice (ZIBSIP) is a Technical, Vocational and Entrepreneurship Training (TEVET) institution under the Ministry of Higher Education (MoHE) that operates as a government-aided institution under the governance of a Management Board. ZIBSIP offers training through full-time, part-time, and distance learning among other learning pathways.

It was established in 1993 in Kitwe to provide training in business courses and other related programs. The institution started with only two programs: Diploma in Secretarial and Certificate in Accounts and Business Studies (CABS), and grew to offer more than 15 business courses by the year 2000. In 2014, the institution introduced seven (7) education courses.

The institution currently has approximately 78 members of staff, 60 on three-year contracts and 18 on part-time. ZIBSIP intends to start offering Degree programs in Commercial Studies and Business Administration shortly.
